Lifeguard AI Agent – Emergency Wristband

Tourism Safety Project

Problem

  • Tourists face drowning, falls, disasters
  • Cannot always shout or use phones

Solution

  • Siren + Strobe alerts nearby people
  • Sends SOS to lifeguards (no mobile needed)

Core Functions

  • Pre-alert vibration
  • Siren time limit
  • Multi-sensor confirmation

Wristband

Sensors, Siren, Strobe, Waterproof
